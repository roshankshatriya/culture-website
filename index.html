Our Legacy

Preserving the
Sacred Art of India

For over two decades, Sanskriti has been a sanctuary for those seeking to immerse themselves in the profound beauty of Indian classical arts. Our journey began with a simple mission: to nurture the next generation of artists through the time-honored Guru-Shishya tradition.

We believe that dance and music are more than just performance; they are a spiritual journey and a living heritage that connects us to our roots.
